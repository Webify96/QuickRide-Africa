Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Who We Are

QuickRide Africa ("we", "us", "our") is a professional ground transportation company operating in Southern Africa. Our contact details are: Email: booking@quickride.africa · Phone: +27 74 270 5488.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or submit a transport request, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name, company name, and contact details (email, phone, WhatsApp)
  • Country of residence
  • Journey details including pickup/drop-off locations, dates, and times
  • Passenger information and luggage details
  • Flight information for airport transfers
  • Vehicle preferences and special requests
  • Any uploaded itinerary documents

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and respond to your transport quote request
  • Coordinate and manage your transport booking
  • Communicate with you about your booking or enquiry
  • Provide customer support

We do not use your information for marketing without your explicit consent. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except where necessary to fulfil your transport booking (such as our drivers or operations team).

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with applicable legal obligations. Quote requests that do not proceed to booking are retained for a reasonable period to allow for follow-up, after which they are securely deleted.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable South African data protection legislation (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at booking@quickride.africa.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal technical cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies at this time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at booking@quickride.africa or call +27 74 270 5488.
